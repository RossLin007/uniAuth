## English Version

### 1. Introduction

UniAuth ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our unified authentication service ("Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Personal Information

When you register for or use our Service, we may collect the following personal information:

**Required Information:**
- **Phone Number**: Used for account verification and login via SMS authentication
- **Email Address**: Used for account verification, login, and communication

**Optional Information:**
- **Display Name/Nickname**: Shown in your profile and to third-party applications you authorize
- **Profile Picture**: Displayed in your profile
- **Password**: If you choose email/password authentication (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

#### 2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type and version
- **IP Address**: Used for security, rate limiting, and geographic analytics
- **Access Logs**: Date and time of access, actions performed
- **Session Information**: Login sessions, device identifiers
- **OAuth Authorization Data**: Third-party applications you have authorized

#### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

If you choose to link your account with third-party services (Google, GitHub, WeChat, etc.), we may receive:

- Your name and profile picture
- Email address
- Unique identifier from the third-party service
- Other information you have made publicly available

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

#### 3.1 Service Provision
- Authenticate your identity
- Provide access to your account
- Process login requests
- Manage your sessions across devices
- Enable third-party application authorization

#### 3.2 Security
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Monitor for suspicious activity
- Enforce rate limits to prevent abuse
- Maintain audit logs for security purposes

#### 3.3 Communication
- Send verification codes via SMS or email
- Notify you of security events (new logins, password changes)
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send important service updates (opt-out available for non-essential communications)

#### 3.4 Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Develop new features

### 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

#### 4.1 Third-Party Applications

When you authorize a third-party application to access your account, we share:
- Your user ID
- Information you explicitly consent to share (name, email, phone, etc.)
- Only the permissions (scopes) you approve

We do NOT share:
- Your password
- Your authentication tokens
- Information about other applications you've authorized

#### 4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

| Service Type | Provider | Data Shared | Purpose |
|-------------|----------|-------------|---------|
| Database | Supabase | All user data | Data storage |
| SMS | Tencent Cloud | Phone number | Verification codes |
| Caching | Upstash Redis | Session data | Performance |
| Error Tracking | Sentry | Error logs | Debugging |

All service providers are contractually obligated to:
- Keep your information confidential
- Use it only for the services they provide to us
- Implement appropriate security measures

#### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Government requests
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- To enforce our Terms of Service

#### 4.4 Business Transfers

If UniAuth is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Account Information | Until account deletion | Service operation |
| Verification Codes | 5 minutes | Security |
| Access Tokens | 1 hour | Authentication |
| Refresh Tokens | 30 days | Session management |
| Audit Logs | 90 days | Security & compliance |
| OAuth Authorizations | Until revoked | Service operation |

After account deletion:
- Personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics may be retained
- Legal hold data may be retained as required

### 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

#### 6.1 Technical Measures
- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted using TLS 1.2+
- **Encryption at Rest**: Sensitive data encrypted in database
- **Password Hashing**: bcrypt with appropriate cost factor
- **Token Security**: Secure random generation, short expiration
- **Rate Limiting**: Protection against brute-force attacks
- **PKCE**: Required for OAuth public clients

#### 6.2 Operational Measures
- Access controls and authentication for all systems
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Employee security training
- Incident response procedures
- Secure development practices

#### 6.3 Compliance
- OWASP Top 10 security practices
- Regular dependency vulnerability scanning
- Security headers (HSTS, CSP, etc.)

### 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

#### 7.1 Access
You can view your personal information through your account settings or by contacting us.

#### 7.2 Correction
You can update your personal information through your account settings.

#### 7.3 Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data through account settings or by contacting us. Note:
- Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- It may take up to 30 days to fully remove all data
- Some information may be retained for legal compliance

#### 7.4 Portability
You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

#### 7.5 Revocation
You can revoke third-party application access at any time through your account settings.

#### 7.6 Opt-Out
You can opt out of non-essential communications through your account settings.

### 8. Cookies and Tracking

Our Service uses minimal cookies and tracking:

| Cookie/Storage | Purpose | Duration |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| uniauth_access_token | Authentication | Session/1 hour |
| uniauth_refresh_token | Session refresh | 30 days |
| uniauth_pkce_verifier | OAuth PKCE flow | Session |

We do NOT use:
- Third-party advertising cookies
- Cross-site tracking
- Analytics cookies (beyond essential metrics)

### 9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately.

### 10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:
- Standard contractual clauses
- Data processing agreements
- Security measures described in this policy

### 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending you an email notification (for material changes)

Changes become effective 30 days after posting, unless a longer period is required by law.

### 12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

**Data Protection Officer**
- Email: privacy@uniauth.example.com
- Address: [Your Company Address]

**For General Inquiries**
- Email: support@uniauth.example.com

**Response Time**: We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.

## 中文版本

### 1. 简介

UniAuth（"我们"或"我方"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了在您使用我们的统一身份认证服务（"服务"）时，我们如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的信息。

请仔细阅读本隐私政策。使用本服务即表示您同意按照本政策收集和使用信息。

### 2. 我们收集的信息

#### 2.1 个人信息

当您注册或使用我们的服务时，我们可能会收集以下个人信息：

**必需信息：**
- **手机号码**：用于通过短信认证进行账户验证和登录
- **电子邮箱**：用于账户验证、登录和通信

**可选信息：**
- **显示名称/昵称**：显示在您的个人资料中以及您授权的第三方应用中
- **头像**：显示在您的个人资料中
- **密码**：如果您选择邮箱/密码认证（使用行业标准哈希安全存储）

#### 2.2 使用信息

当您使用我们的服务时，我们会自动收集某些信息：

- **设备信息**：设备类型、操作系统、浏览器类型和版本
- **IP 地址**：用于安全、速率限制和地理分析
- **访问日志**：访问日期和时间、执行的操作
- **会话信息**：登录会话、设备标识符
- **OAuth 授权数据**：您已授权的第三方应用

#### 2.3 来自第三方的信息

如果您选择将账户与第三方服务（Google、GitHub、微信等）关联，我们可能会收到：

- 您的姓名和头像
- 电子邮箱
- 第三方服务的唯一标识符
- 您已公开的其他信息

### 3. 我们如何使用您的信息

我们将收集的信息用于以下目的：

#### 3.1 服务提供
- 验证您的身份
- 提供账户访问
- 处理登录请求
- 管理您在多设备上的会话
- 启用第三方应用授权

#### 3.2 安全
- 防止未经授权的访问
- 检测和防止欺诈和滥用
- 监控可疑活动
- 执行速率限制以防止滥用
- 维护安全审计日志

#### 3.3 通信
- 通过短信或电子邮件发送验证码
- 通知您安全事件（新登录、密码更改）
- 回复您的咨询和支持请求
- 发送重要服务更新（非必要通信可选择退订）

#### 3.4 改进
- 分析使用模式以改进服务
- 调试和修复技术问题
- 开发新功能

### 4. 信息共享和披露

#### 4.1 第三方应用

当您授权第三方应用访问您的账户时，我们会共享：
- 您的用户 ID
- 您明确同意共享的信息（姓名、邮箱、手机等）
- 仅限您批准的权限（范围）

我们不共享：
- 您的密码
- 您的认证令牌
- 关于您已授权的其他应用的信息

#### 4.2 服务提供商

我们可能会与代表我们执行服务的第三方服务提供商共享信息：

| 服务类型 | 提供商 | 共享数据 | 用途 |
|---------|--------|---------|------|
| 数据库 | Supabase | 所有用户数据 | 数据存储 |
| 短信 | 腾讯云 | 手机号码 | 验证码 |
| 缓存 | Upstash Redis | 会话数据 | 性能 |
| 错误追踪 | Sentry | 错误日志 | 调试 |

所有服务提供商都有合同义务：
- 保守您信息的机密性
- 仅将其用于向我们提供的服务
- 实施适当的安全措施

#### 4.3 法律要求

如果法律要求，我们可能会披露您的信息，包括响应：
- 法院命令或传票
- 政府请求
- 保护我们的权利、隐私、安全或财产
- 执行我们的服务条款

#### 4.4 业务转让

如果 UniAuth 涉及合并、收购或资产出售，您的信息可能会被转让。在您的信息被转让并受不同隐私政策约束之前，我们将发出通知。

### 5. 数据保留

| 数据类型 | 保留期限 | 原因 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 账户信息 | 直到账户删除 | 服务运营 |
| 验证码 | 5 分钟 | 安全 |
| 访问令牌 | 1 小时 | 认证 |
| 刷新令牌 | 30 天 | 会话管理 |
| 审计日志 | 90 天 | 安全与合规 |
| OAuth 授权 | 直到撤销 | 服务运营 |

账户删除后：
- 个人数据在 30 天内删除
- 匿名分析数据可能会保留
- 法律保留数据可能会根据要求保留

### 6. 数据安全

我们实施行业标准安全措施来保护您的信息：

#### 6.1 技术措施
- **传输加密**：所有传输数据使用 TLS 1.2+
- **静态加密**：敏感数据在数据库中加密
- **密码哈希**：使用适当成本因子的 bcrypt
- **令牌安全**：安全随机生成，短有效期
- **速率限制**：防止暴力攻击
- **PKCE**：OAuth 公共客户端必需

#### 6.2 运营措施
- 所有系统的访问控制和认证
- 定期安全审计和渗透测试
- 员工安全培训
- 事件响应程序
- 安全开发实践

#### 6.3 合规
- OWASP Top 10 安全实践
- 定期依赖项漏洞扫描
- 安全头部（HSTS、CSP 等）

### 7. 您的权利

您对个人信息享有以下权利：

#### 7.1 访问权
您可以通过账户设置或联系我们查看您的个人信息。

#### 7.2 更正权
您可以通过账户设置更新您的个人信息。

#### 7.3 删除权
您可以通过账户设置或联系我们删除您的账户及所有相关数据。请注意：
- 删除是永久性的，无法撤销
- 完全删除所有数据可能需要长达 30 天
- 某些信息可能因法律合规要求而保留

#### 7.4 数据可携权
您可以请求以机器可读格式获取您的数据副本。

#### 7.5 撤销权
您可以随时通过账户设置撤销第三方应用的访问权限。

#### 7.6 退订权
您可以通过账户设置退订非必要通信。
